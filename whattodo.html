Got Malware?

"If You're Reading This It's Too Late"

Image from CSO Online

In the likelihood that one does get Malware on their computer, there are steps that can be taken to help make efforts to remove the threat from the computer or affected device. These methods do not gurantee success in regards to fully removing the virus but in the case, the virus remains you would either have to take it to a professional company that handles viruses or you would have to trash the infected part or the device as a whole.

  • Windows

    Option 1

  1. Enter Safe Mode
  2. Select Troubleshoot
  3. Advanced Options
  4. Startup Settings
  5. Restart

    Option 2

  1. Delete Temporary Files
  2. Disk Cleanup

Option 3

  1. Download a Virus Scanner
  2. Perform a Virus Scan

  • Mac

Options

  • MacDefender
  • MacProtector
  • MacSecurity

After you successfully remove the virus you are going to want to recover any damanged files that were effected by the virus. In addition, you should make backups for your programs in case something like this were to happen again. You would have the option of just reverting back to the version prior to the virus. The next step should be purchasing an antivirus protection software and making sure it is kept up to date to ensure computer integrity.
